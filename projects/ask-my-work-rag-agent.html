Ask My Work (RAG Portfolio Agent)

A retrieval-based assistant that answers questions about my projects, skills, and impact with grounded summaries.

Impact: recruiter Q&A in seconds Role: Owner Stack: RAG · Next.js · Vector DB

The Problem

Recruiters scan fast. I needed a way for anyone to query my work like a product—instantly and accurately.

Constraints

Grounded answers (avoid hallucinations) Fast UX (streamed responses) Simple content management

Approach

Index case studies + resume text → embed → retrieve top chunks → generate response with citations (optional).

Chunking strategy Retrieval evaluation Fallback prompts

Results

Time-to-answer: < 5s Lower recruiter friction AI-native brand signal
